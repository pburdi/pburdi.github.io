What is Yelp Ambience data and Why is it important?

  • Ambience is a business attribute located in the Yelp business data and represents the “ambience” of a particular establishment based on the reviews or tips provided by a client or user of this establishment.

  • It is important as a marketing tool for use by Yelp and their business partners to focus promotions or gather data on specific categories of establishments that produce an “ambience” type.

How does the Ambience Marketing Study Help?

  • Yelp contains many categories from Hotels to Restaurants, from Auto Repair to Human Repair, each of these categories may have “ambience”“ data associated with the establishment.
  • The current "ambience” attributes in the Yelp data are:
[1] "romantic" "intimate" "classy"   "hipster"  "divey"    "touristy"
[7] "trendy"   "upscale"  "casual"
  • Combining the Business Category and Ambience attributes help you identify your target clients!
